The Financial Decision-Making Experiment

In this experiment, you will decide how much to save during a simulated life.

Your life will have two phases: a working phase (10 periods) and a rest phase (5 periods). In the working phase, you will work, earn income, and decide how much to save. In the rest phase, you will not earn any income.

You will have two lives.
