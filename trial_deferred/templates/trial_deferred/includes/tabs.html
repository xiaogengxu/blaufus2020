{% blocktrans %}In this experiment, you will decide how much to save during a simulated life.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Your life will have two phases: a working phase (10 periods) and a rest phase (5 periods). In the working phase, you will work, earn income, and decide how much to save. In the rest phase, you will not earn any income.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}You will have two lives.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}At the beginning of each period of the working phase, you will work on a simple task.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}For each submitted task you will receive a fixed Wage of 480 points.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Your Wage will be subject to a Tax of 40%. The Tax will be deducted from your Wage.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Your Net Wage will be your Wage minus Tax.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Following the work task, at each working period, you will decide how much of your Net Wage to put on Savings for the rest phase.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Your Savings will earn 5% interest per period.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Since you do not pay taxes on Savings, you will receive 40% of the amount that you decide to save in each period as a Refund, which will be added automatically to your Reward in each period. For example, if you save 100 points in one period, you will receive 40 points as a Tax Refund in that period.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}You will receive your Savings including accumulated interest back during the rest phase. The computer will automatically calculate equal Withdraw per each period of the rest phase.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Savings Withdraws in the rest phase are subject to a tax of 40%.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In working periods, your Reward will be the difference between your Net Wage and your Savings, plus the tax refund.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In rest periods, your Reward will equal the Withdraw minus its Tax of 40%.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}In each period, your Reward will be automatically calculated.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}For your final Reward, the computer will randomly select one period of one of your two lives. The Reward (in points) in that period will be exchanged for Euros at a rate of € 1.00 = 25 points.{% endblocktrans %}
